Pricing FAQs

Can I try before committing?

Yes! We offer a 14-day free trial for the Starter plan. No credit card required. You'll have full access to one AI tool of your choice.

What if we need more students?

You can upgrade at any time. Additional students are charged at HK$3.88/student/month on Professional plans.

Is there a setup fee?

No setup fees for Starter and Professional plans. Enterprise plans may have a one-time integration fee depending on your requirements.

Do you offer discounts?

Yes! Annual plans save 17%. We also offer discounts for: new schools (20% first year), school networks (volume), and EDB-registered charities.
