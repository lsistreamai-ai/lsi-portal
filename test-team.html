Our Story

LSI started in 2015 when Patrick Siu, a veteran educator, noticed that Hong Kong teachers spent more time on paperwork than actual teaching. He asked: "What if AI could handle the repetitive tasks, so teachers can focus on what matters most — the students?"

Today, LSI serves 20+ schools across Hong Kong, from elite DSE schools to international institutions. Our team combines decades of classroom experience with cutting-edge AI development.

Our mission: Give every Hong Kong student access to world-class AI-powered education, in both English and Chinese.
