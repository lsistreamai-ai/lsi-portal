🚀 Getting Started

How quickly can we get started? +
Most schools can start using LSI within 1-2 weeks after signing up. This includes account setup, teacher onboarding, and initial training. For urgent needs, we can expedite to 48 hours for the Starter plan.
Do we need special hardware or software? +
No! LSI works on any device with a web browser - computers, tablets, or smartphones. For Meta School (VR/AR) features, we recommend basic VR headsets, but even Google Cardboard works for beginners.
What support do you provide during onboarding? +
All plans include:
  • Video training tutorials
  • PDF user guides (English & Chinese)
  • WhatsApp support during business hours
Professional and Enterprise plans include live training sessions and a dedicated account manager.

🔒 Security & Privacy

Is student data safe with LSI? +
Absolutely. Student data security is our top priority:
  • All data encrypted at rest and in transit (AES-256)
  • Servers located in Hong Kong (data residency)
  • Compliant with PDPO (Personal Data Privacy Ordinance)
  • No data shared with third parties
  • Regular security audits by independent firms
We can sign your school's DPA (Data Processing Agreement) upon request.
Can students access AI to cheat? +
We've built safeguards to prevent misuse:
  • Learning Buddy guides students to answers rather than giving them directly
  • Teachers can monitor all AI interactions
  • Content filters aligned with EDB guidelines
  • Usage logs for accountability
The goal is to teach students HOW to learn, not to do their work for them.
Who owns the content and data? +
Your school owns all content created using LSI (lesson plans, assessments, student work). LSI does not use your data to train AI models. You can export and delete all data at any time.

💰 Pricing

Is there a free trial? +
Yes! We offer 14 days free access to one AI tool of your choice. No credit card required. You can try all features with real students and teachers.
What payment methods do you accept? +
We accept bank transfer, cheque, and credit card. Official invoices are provided for school procurement. PO (Purchase Order) accepted for government schools.
Can we cancel anytime? +
Monthly plans can be cancelled with 30 days notice. Annual plans receive prorated refunds. We'll help export your data before cancellation at no charge.

⚙️ Technical

Does LSI integrate with our existing systems? +
Yes! We integrate with:
  • eClass (student data sync)
  • Google Classroom (assignments)
  • Microsoft Teams
  • School websites (SSO available)
Custom integrations available for Enterprise plans.
What happens if internet goes down? +
LSI requires internet connection to function. However:
  • Auto-save prevents data loss
  • Mobile app works offline for students (syncs when connected)
  • 99.9% uptime guaranteed (SLA)
Is LSI available in Chinese? +
Yes! The entire platform is available in Traditional Chinese (繁體中文) and English. AI responses are bilingual by default for Hong Kong schools.
