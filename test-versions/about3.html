Who We Are

Language Services International (PET) Ltd. has been at the forefront of AI education innovation in Hong Kong since 2015. We specialize in developing cutting-edge AI tools for teachers and students, aligned with DSE and IB curricula.

Our mission is to bridge the gap between technology and education, making AI-powered learning accessible to schools across Hong Kong. We work closely with educators to design and implement effective learning platforms tailored to diverse needs.
