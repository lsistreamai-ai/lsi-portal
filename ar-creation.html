Common Questions

How do I access the scan feature for AR books?

To access the scan feature, open the AR app on your device and navigate to the "Scan" section. Point your camera at the book cover or the designated AR marker to begin the scanning process.

Can I scan multiple books at once?

No, the app can only scan one book at a time. Make sure to scan each book individually for the best results. Once you finish with one book, you can proceed to scan the next.

What should I do if the scan doesn't work?

If the scan doesn't work, ensure that you are in a well-lit area and that the book cover is clearly visible. Try repositioning the book or adjusting the distance between the camera and the cover. If issues persist, restart the app and try again.

What features can I access after scanning an AR book?

After scanning an AR book, you can access interactive elements such as quizzes, additional content related to the book, and multimedia features like videos or animations that enhance your reading experience.
